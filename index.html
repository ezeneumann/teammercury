Emerging Themes & Trends

 

  • Authenticity continues to win, with brands investing in creator content, live experiences, and organic engagement rather than relying solely on traditional ads.
  • Economic pressure is elevating "value + joy" messaging, especially in food service, QSR, and CPG—positioning products as accessible treats or everyday essentials.
  • AI-driven personalization is expanding, especially for B2B and service-driven brands, invoking relevance but also prompting consumer scrutiny over data use and privacy.
  • Trust-building and transparency are overt responses to digital fatigue and skepticism—especially among younger consumers—reshaping not just what brands say, but how and where they communicate it.
